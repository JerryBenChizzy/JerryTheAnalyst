Web Scraping, Ingredient Matching (Python) and Data Cleaning

I developed a Python-based web scraping and data analysis project focused on skincare products from iHerb.com. Due to geo-location restrictions that blocked direct access to the site, I worked around this limitation by saving the HTML blocks of individual product pages and then using Python (BeautifulSoup, Pandas) to parse and extract relevant information such as product name, brand, price, description, page URLs, image URLs, and full ingredient lists. After cleaning the data, I implemented a matching logic to identify and group products that share two or more key ingredients, providing insights into product similarities based on active components. The final deliverables included a clean dataset, a grouped ingredient match table, and a fully commented Google Colab notebook demonstrating the process. This project highlights my skills in overcoming scraping challenges, as well as in data extraction, cleaning, and analysis.
